Eyevos — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026 · Eyevos is a free, points-only social prediction game. It involves no real money and no gambling of any kind. Suitable for ages 10+.

The short version

We collect the minimum needed to run a friends prediction game: your sign-in email, the display name and avatar you choose, the groups you join, and the predictions/votes/points you make. We never sell your data, never handle money, and never show ads. You can delete your account any time.

What we store

  • Account: your email address (used only to sign you in via Google or a magic link) and a profile id.
  • Profile: the display name and avatar (emoji or image link) you choose.
  • Game data: groups you create or join, predictions you make, your votes, and your points/scores.

Data is stored with Supabase (Postgres + Auth). Row-Level Security restricts your group data to members of your groups. We do not store payment information because there are no payments.

What we do NOT do

  • No real money, deposits, withdrawals, or purchases — points have no cash value.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data with advertisers.
  • No tracking ads, no third-party analytics profiling.
  • No public exposure of your email or phone — they are never shown to other players.

Children (ages 10+)

Eyevos is designed to be appropriate for ages 10 and up: points only, no money, no gambling, and content rules against demeaning material. We collect only what is needed to play. If you are a parent and want a child's account and data removed, contact us below and we will delete it.

Sharing

Invite links contain only a group name and a join code — never personal information. WhatsApp shares contain only the prediction text and a link you chose to share.

Your choices

  • Edit your display name and avatar any time in the profile screen.
  • Leave a group, or request full account + data deletion by emailing us.
